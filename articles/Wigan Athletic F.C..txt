Wigan Athletic F.C.
Wigan Athletic Football Club is an English football club. The club plays in the Championship, currently managed by Owen Coyle. They play their home games at the DW Stadium. In 2013 they won the FA Cup, and so will play in the UEFA Europa League in the season 2013/14. A few days later they were relegated from the English Premier League to the Football League Championship, becoming the first FA Cup-winning side to be relegated in the the same season. Manager Roberto Martinez left the club shortly after to join Everton FC. Owen Coyle replaced him.

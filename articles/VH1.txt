VH1
VH1 (Video Hits One) is an American television network. It is a sister station to the groundbreaking MTV.
VH1 shows more of the classic videos, alongside chart-toppers, whereas MTV's focus is on the current hits. VH1 also shows You're Cut Off! on Mondays for 3 weeks and Wednesdays for 5 weeks. It started airing on June 9, 2010 (2010-06-09). Casting has been renewed for a 2nd season.
